About Us



At Thé Vert, we are inspired by the beauty and tradition of Japanese tea culture. Rooted in the philosophy of Ichigo Ichie — “one time, one meeting” — we believe that every cup of matcha is a unique moment to be experienced and appreciated.

Our goal is to bring you high-quality matcha in all its forms, from traditional preparations to modern drinks and desserts. Each product is crafted with care, balancing authenticity with creativity.

At Thé Vert, it’s more than just tea — it’s about slowing down, savoring the moment, and creating memories, one cup at a time.


Our Mision



To share the art of matcha by offering high-quality products that bring calm, balance, and enjoyment to every moment.

Our Vision



To create a space where every cup reflects “one time, one meeting” — a unique and meaningful experience through matcha.
